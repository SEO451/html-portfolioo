I'm A Digital Marketer

  1. Skills For An SEO Executive

    1. Keyword Research
      • Ability to find relevant, high-traffic, and low-competition keywords.
      • Understanding of search intent.
    2. On-Page SEO
      • Knowledge of optimizing titles, meta descriptions, headers, image alt tags, and internal linking.
      • Basic HTML knowledge (for editing tags, headings, etc.).
    3. Off-Page SEO
      • Link building strategies (guest posting, outreach, social bookmarking, etc.).
      • Understanding of domain authority and backlink quality.
    4. Technical SEO
      • Crawlability, indexation, and sitemap creation.
      • Robots.txt optimization.
      • Site speed optimization.
      • Mobile-friendliness.
      • Canonical tags and duplicate content issues.
    5. Content Strategy
      • Ability to guide or create SEO-friendly content.
      • Understanding of LSI keywords and content relevance.
    6. Analytical Skills
      • Track traffic, rankings, and user behavior using tools.
      • Measure and interpret SEO performance.
    7. Basic Coding Knowledge
      • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript basics.
    8. Communication & Reporting
      • Ability to prepare reports and present SEO insights to clients or managers.
      • Strong writing and verbal communication skills.
